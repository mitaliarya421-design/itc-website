About

Institute Technical Council

itc.
Institute
Technical
Council

The Institute Technical Council (ITC) is the central body for all technical activities within the institute. It plays a crucial role in fostering innovation, technical skills, and a culture of creativity among students.


ITC serves as a platform where ideas are transformed into reality. By providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities, the council empowers students to explore, build, and innovate across diverse domains of technology.

What ITC Does

ITC organizes workshops, competitions, and projects, bringing students together to collaborate across technical domains and gain real-world experience.

❓

Structure of ITC

ITC includes multiple clubs and teams across domains like web development, robotics, aeromodelling, and AI, working together to build innovative projects and organize technical events.

⚙️

Mission

To cultivate a strong technical culture by enabling students to learn, innovate, and apply their skills to solve real-world problems. and to create a dynamic and inclusive environment where technology and creativity come together to drive innovation and excellence.

🎯

With numerous clubs, teams, and events, ITC impacts thousands of students every year. It provides opportunities to work on projects, participate in competitions, and collaborate with like-minded individuals. The council continues to inspire students to push boundaries and achieve excellence in the field of technology.
